What We Offer

World-Class Facilities

Every element of our campus is designed to create the optimal environment for learning and competition.

Main Training Hall

A climate-controlled, distraction-free hall with 40 professional-grade DGT chess sets, demo boards, and padded seating for extended sessions.

Digital Analysis Lab

Workstations powered by Stockfish 17 engine, ChessBase, and Lichess integrations for deep game analysis and opening preparation.

Tournament Arena

An FIDE-compliant tournament area with wall clocks, demonstration boards, and live result display — ideal for internal championships.

Dedicated Coaching

Small-group coaching sessions led by certified instructors, focused on tactics, openings, and structured skill progression.

Online Classes

Our online classes are conducted with live boards and screen-shared analysis, ensuring clear instruction for remote students.

Junior Outreach

School visits and beginner workshops that bring chess fundamentals to young learners across Cuddalore and surrounding areas.
