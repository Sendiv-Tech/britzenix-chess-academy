Who We Are

Eighteen Years of Moving Pieces, Changing Lives

Britzenix Chess Academy was founded in 2006 by International Master Rajesh Venkatesh in a modest room in Anna Nagar, Chennai. What started as weekend coaching for a handful of eager students quickly became a movement. Within three years, our students were winning district and state competitions. Today, we operate two campuses, offer hybrid online-offline training, and have produced over 1,200 players — including national champions and FIDE-rated masters.

We believe chess is more than a game. It is a discipline that cultivates patience, foresight, emotional resilience, and analytical rigour — skills that serve our students throughout their lives, far beyond the board.

🌟

Our Mission

To make world-class chess education accessible to every aspiring player, nurturing strategic thinkers who excel in competition and in life.

🔭

Our Vision

To be South India's foremost chess institution, producing national champions and internationally rated players while advancing the culture of chess in Tamil Nadu.

💎

Our Values

Rigour, integrity, curiosity, and growth mindset. We demand excellence but cultivate it through encouragement, analysis, and joy of the game.
